I'm an aspiring veterinarian who is currently studying Biology in University of California: Riverside. Right now, I am part of Dr. Kurt Anderson's Ecology Lab investigating protist ecology. At the research lab, I am responsible for sampling different cultures containing different protist species that act as either predators or prey. Some of the common protists that we actively investigate are Euploides (which are a predator species) and Tetras (which are prey species). Ecological relationships in protists greatly reflect dynamic relationships in the real world, and thus studies using protists are great reflections about our own ecological system. Population growth, relationships, and success are all vital parts in this lab. I am a sophomore as of 2020, and will be a junior this coming September.
